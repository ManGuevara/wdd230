White Space and Clean Design

Twiter

twiter.com

In this page, we can see a good use of white space. For example, in the buttons there is enough white space for the padding, which is white space also. Also, the logo of the bird is using white space making a good contrast with the background. Finally, white space gives the elements a nice alignment to the center of the page.

Contrast

Prime Video

primevideo.com

In this page, there is a good contrast between the black background and the white color text. Also, the images have a nice contrast with the black background. Moreover, the size of the first picture at the top gives a nice contrast with the other small pictures, we could say also that there is a visual hierarchy between them.

Visual Hierarchy

Cambridge University

www.cam.ac.uk

In this page, we can see that the arrangements of components is in descending order of importance because it begins with the picture and then each element is describing the image, like topic, the info of the person, and then buttons. Also, the typography and style creates a relevant hierarchy from top to bottom.
