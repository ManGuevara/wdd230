Membership levels

NP membership (Cost: Free)

This membership is for non profit organizations and there is no fee. Be part of our community and you not only will be able to attend the weekly meetings but you will have the most recent news and training by our team.

Bronze membership (Cost: $100)

On this membershipYou will be able to access the weekly meetings, you will have the most recent news and training by our team. On top of that you will get one free class in any subject you are interested in.

Silver membership (Cost: $150)

On this membership on top of bronze you will have some disccounts bonnus on the neighboorhood stores.Also, you will have one free post on our website for one week.

Gold membership (Cost:$250)

On this membership you will have unlimited acces to our events , plus permanent advice from our staff or lawyers on your bussiness issues.on top of that you will have permanent assistance from our partners on housing reparements.
